Who We Are

India's Apex Body for Plumbing Professionals

Indian Plumbing Association (IPA) is an NGO and the apex body of plumbing professionals in India. Established in 1993, IPA aims to promote the development of the plumbing and building services industry.

IPA membership is open to everyone engaged directly or indirectly in the construction industry. The association has over 12,000+ members across the country, representing every segment — from building consultancy, manufacturing, and contracting to trading, academia, and architecture.

Through 28 national chapters, IPA delivers skill development, technical standards, world-class events, and industry advocacy — building a better India through better plumbing.
